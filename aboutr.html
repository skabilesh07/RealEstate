ABOUT REAL ESTATE

  • Real estate refers to property consisting of land and the buildings on it, along with its natural resources such as crops, minerals, or water.
  • Real estate is a significant component of the economy and plays a crucial role in various aspects of our lives.

Why should we invest in Real Estate?

  • Investing in real estate can be an attractive option for several reasons, although it's essential to note that any investment comes with risks and requires careful consideration.
  • The tangible nature of property, tax benefits, and the ability to exert control over the asset make real estate an appealing option for investors.

The consequences of not investing in Real Estate.

  • Inflation Hedge Absence: Real estate is often viewed as a hedge against inflation. Without it, your investment portfolio might be less resilient to the eroding effects of rising prices.
  • Diversification Gap: Real estate is often considered a diversification tool in an investment portfolio. Without it, your portfolio might be more susceptible to the performance fluctuations of other asset classes.
